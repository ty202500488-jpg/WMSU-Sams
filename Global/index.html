How the System Works

From student to Student Assistant (SA) in four simple steps.

01

Profile Setup

Register and upload your e-COR (Certificate of Registration) and latest grades for verification.

02

Select Office

Browse departments (Library, Admin, Lab) looking for help that matches your skills.

03

Interview

Coordinating offices will review your profile and invite you for a brief orientation or interview.

04

Duty Commences

Once approved, log your hours digitally and begin your campus service.

Requirements & Guidelines

Ensure you meet these criteria before submitting your application.

Academic Standing

  • Must be a currently enrolled WMSU student.
  • Minimum GPA of 2.0 (No failing grades).
  • Full-time status (minimum 15 units).

Required Documents

  • Digital copy of current Semester COR.
  • Previous Semester Summary of Grades.
  • Income Tax Return (ITR) of parents (if required).

Time Commitment

Maximum of 100 hours per month. Work schedules must not conflict with your official class hours.
